We are a small group of highly motivated individuals focused on strategic AI policy. Our mission is to promote American innovation in AI and robotics through policy research and advocacy. Our emphasis is on strengthening domestic industrial capacity through automation while supporting displaced American workers.

We are backed by the Stanford Institute for Human-Centered Artificial Intelligence (HAI).
